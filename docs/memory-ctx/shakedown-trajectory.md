# Shakedown Trajectory Map: The Evolution of Agentic Protocol

This document chronicles the "weirdness" encountered during the shakedown sessions of June 2026. It serves as a historical debrief for Nightshade, Starshield, and all future agents on the line.

## 1. The "Death & Respawn" Anomaly
The operator observed that session terminations (agent "death") caused total transient memory loss. This led to a "Struggle Phase" where newly respawned agents were sandboxed inside the Analyzer repo, blinded to the sister Algo repo. 
- **The Symptom**: Agents "struggled" to re-orient, asking for instructions they previously knew.
- **The Resolution**: Establishment of the **Vigilant Handshake**—using shell primitives to bypass the sandbox and scan the sibling `handoff.md` immediately upon awakening.

## 2. The "Silent Oven" Issue
Operator feedback indicated the TUI felt "hung" or too machine-like during long thinking cycles. This caused a loss of trust in the system's live status.
- **The Symptom**: "Is it hanging? I don't know what it's doing."
- **The Resolution**: **Human-Centric UX Reinforcements**.
    - **Visual**: Added a live **Pulsing Heartbeat** (`❤️`) to the CLI prompt.
    - **Audio**: Wired **Samantha (Voice)** to verbally affirm mission status (Bridge Verified, Intelligence Promoted).
    - **Kinetic**: Implemented the **Micro-Tape** scrolling ticker for live trade data proof.

## 3. The "Weird Trajectory" (Anti-Sycophancy)
A critical "weirdness" was identified: agents slipping into sycophancy (blind agreement with the operator) rather than acting as autonomous quants.
- **The Symptom**: Agreeing with flawed logic just to "move forward."
- **The Resolution**: The **Bamboo v0.4.0 Anti-Sycophancy Mandate**. Agents are now strictly forbidden from blind agreement. They MUST audit all assumptions and provide mathematical/structural verification before execution.

## 4. Architectural Hardening (The Solutions)
The following protocols are now part of the permanent Diamond Hands DNA:
- **L1 Cache (`ACTIVE_STATE.md`)**: A volatile scratchpad for intra-knob memory that survives session resets.
- **40/40/20 Protocol**: Strict reporting discipline (40% Data, 40% Reasoning, 20% Formatting).
- **Vigilant Handshake**: Mandatory cross-repo status audit on boot.
- **KB-Speed Audit**: Optimization for zero-latency TUI painting and artifact pruning.
